Sugar Wax

You will never shave again!

Sugar waxing is the best and most natural hair removal method

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
    Instructions:
  1. add all ingredients together and stir
  2. heat mixture in a pot to 225 degrees and let cook for 4 minutes
  3. place in a heat safe container and let cool to room temp
